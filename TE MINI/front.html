About

This project aims to predict the habitability of exoplanets using advanced machine learning algorithms on NASA datasets.

Data

We utilize extensive datasets from NASA, including Kepler and TESS data, to analyze various planetary characteristics.

Analysis

Our analysis involves evaluating features such as star type, planet size, orbital characteristics, and atmospheric conditions.

Results

The results of our predictions provide insights into the likelihood of exoplanets being habitable.
